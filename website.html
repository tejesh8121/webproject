Project Details
About ServeToday
Everything you need to know before joining our community service project.
Mission

Why We Exist

ServeToday bridges the gap between college students and underserved communities. We believe in learning through serving — developing future leaders who lead with empathy and action.

Who Can Join

Eligibility

  • Any currently enrolled college student
  • Faculty, staff, and alumni
  • Community members aged 16 and above
  • Zero experience required — just a willing heart
Schedule

When We Meet

Activities run every Saturday, 7:00 AM – 12:00 NN.
Planning meetings: Wednesdays, 4:00 PM
Room 204, College Main Building.

Benefits

What You Gain

  • Official community service certificate
  • Leadership & facilitation training
  • NGO partner networking opportunities
  • NSTP & subject credit (where applicable)
Requirements

What to Bring

  • Valid school ID
  • Comfortable clothes suitable for fieldwork
  • Water bottle and packed snacks
  • Positive attitude and open mind
Partners

Who We Work With

We collaborate with local government units, registered NGOs, school districts, and barangay councils to ensure our efforts reach those who need it most.
